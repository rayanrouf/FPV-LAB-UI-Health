Welcome to the Math Game!

Get ready to test your tracking skills in a fun and interactive way! Follow these instructions to start playing.

How to Play

Move your body to control the pointer on the screen and follow the big circle as closely to its center as you can. Quick reflexes will take you far!

Game Levels

  • Easy: Simple slow and steady movement, suitable for beginners.
  • Medium: Includes sudden changes in circle movement direction and higher speed.
  • Hard: More random circle movement with higher speed, for those who dare!

Tips for Success

Practice makes perfect, so don't hesitate to retry and improve your score!
