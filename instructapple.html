Welcome to the Math Game!

Get ready to test your Fruit Catching skills in a fun and interactive way! Follow these instructions to start playing.

How to Play

Move your body to control the basket on the screen and catch the correct fruit. Quick reflexes and keen attention will take you far!

Game Levels

  • Easy: Fruits fall slow, suitable for beginners.
  • Medium: Includes more fruits falling slightly quicker.
  • Hard: Challenges with faster dropping fruits for those who dare!

Tips for Success

Practice makes perfect, so don't hesitate to retry and improve your score!
